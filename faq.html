How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our website and add the items you want to your cart. When you're ready to checkout, click on the cart icon in the top right corner and then click "Checkout". Follow the instructions to enter your shipping and payment information to complete your order.

How can I check the status of my order?

You can check the status of your order by visiting the "Track Order" page and entering your order number and email address. You will also receive email updates about your order status.

What shipping methods do you offer?

We offer several shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days)
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days)
  • International Shipping (7-14 business days)

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and the weight of your order.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days, and customs fees may apply depending on your country's regulations.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Please visit our Shipping & Returns page for more detailed information.

Are your Pokémon cards authentic?

Yes, all our Pokémon cards are 100% authentic. We source our products directly from authorized distributors and manufacturers. We have a strict quality control process to ensure the authenticity of all items we sell.

How do you grade your single cards?

We grade our single cards according to industry standards:

  • Mint (M): Perfect condition with no flaws
  • Near Mint (NM): Almost perfect with minimal wear
  • Excellent (EX): Light wear visible on close inspection
  • Good (G): Moderate wear, still playable
  • Poor (P): Heavy wear, may have creases or damage

The condition of each card is clearly stated in the product description.
