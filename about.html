Our Story

Welcome to MemeTCG, your premier destination for Pokémon cards and collectibles in the Netherlands. Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate Pokémon enthusiasts, we've grown from a small online store to one of the leading Pokémon card retailers in the country.

Our journey began when our founder, a lifelong Pokémon fan, noticed the lack of reliable sources for authentic Pokémon cards in the Netherlands. What started as a hobby quickly turned into a thriving business as more collectors discovered our commitment to quality, authenticity, and fair pricing.

At MemeTCG, we believe that collecting Pokémon cards should be an enjoyable and rewarding experience for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned collector looking for that rare card to complete your set, a player seeking to build a competitive deck, or a parent introducing your child to the world of Pokémon, we're here to help you on your journey.

Our team consists of knowledgeable enthusiasts who are always ready to provide expert advice and assistance. We take pride in our carefully curated selection of products, ensuring that every card we sell is authentic and in the condition described.

Our Mission

At MemeTCG, our mission is to provide collectors and players with authentic Pokémon cards at fair prices, while delivering exceptional customer service and fostering a vibrant community of enthusiasts.

We strive to be more than just a store – we aim to be a trusted resource and community hub for Pokémon fans across the Netherlands and beyond. Through our dedication to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction, we hope to contribute to the growth and enjoyment of the Pokémon Trading Card Game.

Our values include:

  • Authenticity: We guarantee that all our products are 100% authentic.
  • Quality: We carefully inspect and grade every card to ensure it meets our high standards.
  • Transparency: We provide clear and accurate descriptions of all our products.
  • Community: We support and engage with the Pokémon community through events, tournaments, and educational content.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to environmentally friendly practices in our operations.

Thank you for choosing MemeTCG. We look forward to being part of your Pokémon journey!
