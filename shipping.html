Shipping Information

At memTCG, we strive to deliver your Pokémon cards and collectibles as quickly and safely as possible. We understand the excitement of receiving new additions to your collection, and we work hard to ensure your items arrive in perfect condition.

Shipping Methods

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping (Netherlands) 1-3 business days €3.95 (Free for orders over €50)
Express Shipping (Netherlands) Next business day €7.95
Standard Shipping (EU) 3-7 business days €9.95 (Free for orders over €100)
Express Shipping (EU) 2-3 business days €14.95
International Shipping 7-14 business days €19.95 (Free for orders over €150)

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on customs clearance for international orders.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order on our website by visiting the Track Order page.

Packaging

We take great care in packaging your items to ensure they arrive in perfect condition:

  • Single cards are placed in protective sleeves and top loaders
  • Sealed products are wrapped in bubble wrap for extra protection
  • All items are shipped in sturdy cardboard boxes or padded envelopes
  • Fragile items receive additional padding and protection

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient. These charges vary by country and are not included in the shipping cost.

Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, we offer a straightforward returns process.

Return Eligibility

You may return items purchased from memTCG within 30 days of delivery, provided that:

  • Items are in their original condition and packaging
  • Sealed products (booster packs, boxes, etc.) remain sealed and unopened
  • Items show no signs of use or damage
  • You have proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

Important: Due to the collectible nature of our products, we cannot accept returns for opened sealed products (booster packs, boxes, etc.) unless they are defective.

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@memtcg.nl or through our Contact page to request a return authorization
  2. Include your order number and the reason for the return
  3. Our team will provide you with return instructions and a return authorization number
  4. Pack the items securely in their original packaging
  5. Include the return authorization number with your package
  6. Ship the package to the address provided in the return instructions

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

  • For credit/debit card payments, refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
  • For PayPal payments, refunds are usually processed within 2-3 business days
  • Bank transfers may take 3-5 business days to process

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (damaged or incorrect items).

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different product, please follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures the fastest processing time.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you. Please provide photos of the damaged items to help us process your claim more efficiently.
